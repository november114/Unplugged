Our Mission - we want to make you the team you always wanted to be.

Founded in 2012 , Alley Basset immediately recognized the need for immersive exploration of mind-body wellness in a relaxed setting after being hospitalized for complications related to exhaustion. She envisioned it being a place of casual elegance and healthy living with one catch, it's a tech free zone. We offer an unforgettable off-the-grid experience.

At unplugged you'll experience a week of peacefulness in a bungalow outside of Lake Geneva. Take part in our 5 outdoor obstacle courses, designed to bring you and your team closer together. Take a mindful walk along our private beach. Let our retreat facilitator manage your daily activities so you get the chance to rest, and let our private chef team cook up your on demand meals.

The average U.S. adult spends 10 hours and 30 minutes each day interacting with some form of media. The average traveler spends 9 hours on social media over the course of a one-week getaway. In today’s hyper-connected world there is no better time than now to do a digital detox for your mental health and overall wellness. We all need ways to disconnect from technology and reconnect with ourselves, without a 24/7 revolving feed of everyone else’s social media lives, never ending emails, and demanding deadlines. It's safe to say that most of us desperately need a little time to unplug and recharge. We want to inspire people to develop better relationships with technology. Our mission is to make you the team you always wanted to be.

Contact us, because you all need this.
